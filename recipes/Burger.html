Hamburgers

Description

You know? That patty where we add a bunch of healthy foods together and they suddenly become unhealthy?

Ingredients

  • 150g ground beef "seasoned with salt and pepper"
  • Burger buns
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Cheese of preference

How to prepare

Butter and toast your buns on the grill.

Put the meat on the grill 4 minutes on each side

Paste the buns with a sauce "a mix of your favorite condiments" of your preference.

Add the tomato and onion slices to top bun, sauce and pickles to the bottom bun.

Melt the cheese on the burger patty, the heat on the meat is enough to melt it fot you.

Put the patty onto the bottom bun and VOILA!
