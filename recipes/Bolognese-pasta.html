Bolognese Pasta

Description

Your classic Italian Pasta served simply.. Bellisima

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • 5 tomatoes
  • 1 ounce ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Coke

How to prepare

First put the tomatoes into boiling water for 1 minute.

Then peel them and squeeze them into the blender.

Add 3 cloves of garlic and some olio di oliva and blend for the tomato sauce we're gonna add to the Pasta

Add water to the pasta and boil.. but please don't forget to salt the water.

Add 2 cloves of garlic and chopped onions into a hot pan with olive oil

Add the ground beef and stir until the beef is cooked.

Add the tomato sauce to the pan and stir, then add pasta water to the mix, TRUST ME.

Add coke to the pan and give it one last stir. BOOM!
