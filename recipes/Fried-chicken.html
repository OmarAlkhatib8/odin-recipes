Fried Chicken

Description

Fried Chicken.. ahh. Who doesn't love fried chicken, eh?

Chicken covered with a seasoned coat and thrown into hot olio?

Here enjoy the read as you learn my or haha "ChatGPT's".. recipe

Ingredients

Chicken pieces (Drumsticks, wings, breasts or thighs)

  • For the marinade :
  • 4 cups buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

  • For the coating :
  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

How to prepare

1. Let your chicken sit in the marinade for a few hours

2. Dredge the chicken in the flour mix

3. For better crunchiness, we recommend dredging the chicken back into the marinade and back into the flour mix

4. Place in oil at 175Â°C

Fry for 10-12 minutes until golden brown
